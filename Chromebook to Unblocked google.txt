Chromebook to Unblocked google
1. Get to sign in screen
2. Click Sign In with Microsoft
3. Click Sign In Options
4. Click Sign In with GitHub
5. Scroll down and click Docs
6. Scroll down and click Ask the GitHub Community
7. Click the search icon in top right, clear what's already in it and search "google"
8. Click the second result: "google/googletest"
9. Click google next to google/googletest in the top left
10. Click material-design-icons
11. Scroll down and click "https://fonts.google.com/icons"
12. Scroll all the way down (takes a while) and click Terms Of Service in bottom right
13. Click 3 lines in top left
